PodnLearn - Podcast Player & AI Learner

PodnLearn is a Podcast and AI-based e-book learner web application in which the user, particularly, the listener can access a wide range of podcasts with also some of the exclusive indie podcasters. Another feature is “LearnAI”, in which you can upload an e-book, which will convert the text written in it, into an audiobook like audio. The LearnAI will help the people who has difficulties in eyesight.
